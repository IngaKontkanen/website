BigHeart aims to protect all animals to the best of our abilities. We focus on providing aid to homeless animals. During the last 5 years, we have taken approximately 2 000 homeless animals into our care and found new homes for them. Furthermore, we give advice on animal protection and animal care. We invite you to learn more about us and our little friends.
